Our Story

Founded from friendship, built with purpose.

Liore was started by two passionate young girls, Fathima and her friend Tabassum, right after they completed their 11th class. United by a shared dream of creating something that brings healing and confidence to people battling sensitive skin conditions, they turned their vision into a reality.

From their early days of experimenting with natural ingredients at home, to building a brand trusted by patients and loved ones, the journey of Liore reflects resilience, creativity, and care. Their core mission remains: to provide gentle, effective products that truly care for skin and soul.

Today, Liore stands as a symbol of compassion-driven skincare — and it all began with the courage of two young founders, driven by hope and friendship.
